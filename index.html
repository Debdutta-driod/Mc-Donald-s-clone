About McDonald's

Our Story

McDonald's was founded in 1940 by brothers Richard and Maurice McDonald in San Bernardino, California. What started as a small drive-in restaurant has grown into one of the world's leading food service retailers, serving millions of customers daily across more than 100 countries.

Our commitment to quality, service, cleanliness, and value has made us a global leader in the quick-service restaurant industry. We continue to innovate and evolve while staying true to our core values.

Our Values

  • Quality: We're committed to serving the highest quality food and beverages
  • Service: We provide fast, friendly, and accurate service
  • Cleanliness: We maintain clean, comfortable restaurants
  • Value: We offer great value for money
  • Sustainability: We're committed to sustainable practices
